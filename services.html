    Electrician

    The trade focuses on various aspects of electricity such as – wiring (residential, commercial and industrial), soldering, UGCables, panel board wiring, DC and AC machines, home appliances, electrical machines, lighting, electrical installations etc.

    Duration:

    2 years

    Eligibility:

    SSLC and above The trade of Electrician is affiliated to NCVT Govt. of India with the affiliation no DGET 6(11) (24) Tc Dated 08.07.1986. It consists of 2+2 units with 42 seats in each

    Fitter

    The trade is associated with various types of fitting work involved in industries and various organizations. The syllabus focuses on all types of fitting jobs, welding, smithy and forging, marking and drilling, assembling of products in the various industries/ repair and maintenance of machines etc.

    Duration:

    2 years

    Eligibility:

    SSLC and above The trade of Fitter is affiliated to NCVT Govt. of India with the affiliation no. DGET 6(11) (24) Tc Dated 08.07.1986. It consists of 1+1 units with 21 seats in each.

    Draughtsman civil

    The trade focuses on various aspects of planning, surveying and drawing sketches of residential buildings from given data, preparing working drawings of all types of buildings from line sketches in CAD, planning, drawing, estimating, and costing of civil work etc.

    Duration:

    2 years

    Eligibility:

    SSLC and above The trade of Draughtsman Civil is affiliated to NCVT Govt. of India with the affiliation no. DGET 6(11) (24) Tc Dated 08.07.1986. It consists of 2+2 units with 42 seats in each.

    Electronic mechanic

    The trade is all about repairing, maintaining, testing, installing, fixing various types of electronic equipment like television, LED lights, chips, oscilloscope, cell phone, solar system, etc. It also highlights the area of fiber optic communication.

    Duration:

    2 years

    Eligibility:

    SSLC and above The trade of Electronic Mechanic is affiliated to NCVT Govt. of India with the affiliation no. DGET 12/2/1997 Tc Dated 18.06.1997. It consists of 1+1 units with 21 seats in each

    Computer Operator & Programming Assistant (COPA)

    The syllabus of COPA is approved by the government of India in consultation with NCVT. It is a course related to computers including Hardware and Software, Data Entry, Programming languages, Networking, M S Office, Tally, Internet, Database management, Java Script etc.

    Duration:

    1 year

    Eligibility:

    SSLC and above The trade of Computer Operator & Programming Assistant is affiliated to NCVT Govt. of India with the affiliation no. DGET 12/01/1999 Tc Dated 04.11.1999. It consists of 1+1 units with 26 seats in each.
